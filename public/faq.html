Frequently Asked Questions

UranusResource · a Corgan Studio, Inc. spatial intelligence platform
Last updated April 2026
What is Resource Systems?
Resource Systems is a multi-planetary spatial intelligence platform developed by Corgan Studio, Inc. It provides real-time visualization and analysis of planetary data across Earth, the Moon, Mars, the Sun, and every major body in the Solar System, alongside a Milky Way galactic view. Each platform in the suite shares a consistent design language and a unified account for mission tracking, spatial analysis, and cross-body visualization.
What is UranusResource?
UranusResource is a Uranus intelligence platform combining the ice giant’s surface visualization with its 5 major moons and ring system for the sideways-tilted outer planet.
What can I see on the platform?
UranusResource presents Uranus as an interactive 3D system with its extreme axial tilt, the 5 major moons (Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel, Titania, Oberon) on real Kepler orbits, and the Epsilon ring.
Who created Resource Systems?
Resource Systems is developed by Corgan Studio, Inc., an independent research and design studio founded by Jason Corgan Brown focused on spatial intelligence, worldbuilding, and large-scale data visualization systems. The platform integrates scientific datasets into interactive environments for planetary analysis and mission tracking. Resource Systems launched in 2025.
Is there a paid plan?
Yes. A free tier is available alongside paid tiers with expanded data access, analysis tools, and advanced features. Details are available within the platform.
Is it available on mobile?
Yes. UranusResource runs in modern desktop and mobile browsers. Some advanced 3D features perform best on desktop hardware.
